Combining technical expertise with business acumen to drive data-informed decisions

Driven by curiosity and determination, I've transitioned from pure technology to business analytics—a field where I combine my problem-solving skills with strategic thinking to deliver measurable impact.

My mission is to help organizations navigate challenges and capitalize on opportunities through analytics-backed strategies. Every project is an opportunity to learn, grow, and deliver tangible value.
